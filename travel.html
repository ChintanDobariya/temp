    5 AWESOME THINGS TO DO IN BHUTAN

    Sitting on the eastern slopes of the Himalayas, the Land of the Thunder Dragon is a landlocked country bordered by Tibet and India. Bhutan holds a policy of attracting high-value low impact tourism and the country is therefore not your typical backpacker budget destination.
