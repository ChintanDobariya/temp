    Why is Dolgona Coffee getting in trend? What made it called Dalgona?

    In the past few weeks, an old (not new) Dolgona coffee has been trending over TikTok where many people are usually getting aware about it day by day, even I got the news and was super excited to see whats that good in the coffee to be this much viral over social media, let's see!


    Why is this Coffee getting this much Viral?

    You might have seen a couple of pictures of this coffee being posted by people over different social media platforms like Facebook WhatsApp Instagram especially on TikTok.

    Do you know Turmeric Latte? When Haldi Doodh gets its unique name on the Western side of India? Well, the Western has just found Dalgona Coffee and are making it viral over time. Wait Isn't that just plain old Phenti Hui Coffee made by us over this many years?


    But wait why 'Dolgana' name for the Coffee?

    The origins of the coffee are from natives of India, Pakistan and, Macau where its name is whipped coffee or beaten coffee. It became popular when it appeared on a Korean TV show called Pyunstorang a few months ago. Where, actor Jung Il-woo traveled to its origin Macau, tried the drink, and made it viral by giving the nickname Dalgona for its traditional candy in Korean.

    And it was a perfect time to get this Coffee to be viral due to the COVID-19 outbreak worldwide, with people not being able to visit their favorite coffee shop and just trying different kinds of stuff staying at their home. This coffee is getting thousands of video posts over time with different people from different parts are loving it .


    Want to learn how this Coffee is being made?

    This coffee is just a caramel-colored bubbly at the top on a full glass of milk awaited to be stirred together to form a much tempting newly viral food trend: the Dalgona coffee (Whipped coffee).
